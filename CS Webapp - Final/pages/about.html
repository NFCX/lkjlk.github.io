What is DOIT?

DOIT is an app that helps you to organize yourself in order to build habits, achieve your goals or just don't forget what you have to do. It contains all kinds of features and we are sure you will find those that suit you. We know that in many ocasions and specially nowadays with all the distractions we have around us it may get very complicated to do the things you want to do troughout the day, that why we made this app in order to help you with this problem so many people struggle with.
